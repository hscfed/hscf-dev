Curriculum Sale

ALL WELCOME! to the Annual HSCF Used Book & Curriculum Sale.

Whether you need to declutter your homeschool shelves or find some bargains, plan to come to this event.

  • Table space can be booked ahead by signing up. Space is limited to 16 tables, so registrations will be open to members first and then to non-members.
  • Tables are free for members, $10 for non-members.
  • Everyone looks after their own table of items. If you bring items for the FREE area, you must take home any items that are leftover when you leave.
  • Invite friends, family, facebook & homeschool groups! If you choose to bring your children, you agree to supervise them at all times.
  • Doors open at 7:05pm after announcements. Vendors only prior to that.
  • Bring a shopping bag & cash, including small bills & coins please.
  • The HSCF library will be closed.
