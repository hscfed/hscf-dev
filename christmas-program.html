Christmas Program

The HSCF Christmas Program is an annual highlight! Share your songs, skits, poems and recitations with friends and family as we honour the Christ of Christmas together this evening as families.

  • This event is for HSCF performers only, but we encourage performers to invite guests to watch.
  • Sign up is needed ONLY for those participating in the program with info as it will appear in the program bulletin.
  • Please bring a snack to share after the program.
  • Absolutely no food or drink (other than water) is permitted in the sanctuary. Parents - please supervise your children during the food/fellowship time. Help them to be tidy & let's all find opportunity to serve.
