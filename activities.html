Activities

HSCF activities occur on the last Monday of the month from August to May, with Track & Field happening sometime in June.

HSCF Meetings provide special speakers, time to support and socialize, access to the HSCF library and more...

HSCF Events usually include a Christmas Program, Curriculum Sale, Science & Heritage Fair, Poetry Cafe and Track & Field...

Meetings are held at Faith Community Church (10405 142 St NW, Edmonton) and begin at 7:00pm.

An example of a typical HSCF schedule is:

August: Round Table Discussions

September: Annual General Meeting

October: Guest Speaker or Other

November: Guest Speaker or Other

December: Christmas Program

January: Guest Speaker or Other

February: Guest Speaker or Other

March: Curriculum Sale

April: Science & Heritage Fair

May: Poetry Cafe

June: Track & Field
