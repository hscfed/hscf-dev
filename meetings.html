Meetings

HSCF runs a number of meetings where there are no formal events occurring. This is an opportunity to host homeschool speakers, run one-time events, utilize the HSCF Library or simply meet with fellow homeschoolers for a time of sharing and encouragement.

Some of the speakers we've hosted in the past include Therese MacDonald, Dr. Helder, AHEA representatives and various homeschooling through high school, homeschooling with toddlers or homeschooling special needs experts.

Those wanting to experience HSCF to see if it is the right fit for their family are welcome to attend one meeting free of charge... no membership required.
